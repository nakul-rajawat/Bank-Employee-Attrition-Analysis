# 📊 Bank Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data for a bank to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover. Using Python and data analysis techniques, the project performs exploratory data analysis (EDA), feature engineering, statistical analysis, and visualization to generate actionable business insights.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends.
- Identify factors affecting employee retention.
- Study department-wise and tenure-wise attrition.
- Examine relationships between employee demographics and business performance.
- Provide business recommendations based on data.

---

## Dataset

- **Rows:** 10,816
- **Columns:** 15
- Employee records collected over multiple months.

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Key Findings

- Overall employee attrition rate: **22.60%**
- Employees with **0–6 months** of tenure have the highest attrition.
- **HR & Admin** recorded the highest attrition rate.
- Employees receiving income increases were less likely to leave.
- Education level alone was not a strong predictor of business performance.
